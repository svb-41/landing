Welcome new commander!

SVB-41 is a game placing you as a fleet commander, in a Sci-Fi world. Right after the military school, you gain control of a fleet made of ships and drones, and your role is to find the best strategy to win against your opponents. To do it, you have to choose wisely your ships, and equip them with correct AI.

Contrarily to most games, you're not in direct control of your ships, but instead, you're coding Artifical Intelligence and let them make their decisions. It's up to you to find the best AI and the correct strategy!

Storyline

2500. Earth is now completely under human control, and humanity are looking for the stars. Megacorporations are now building so efficient ships, anyone can start space explorations. Join a faction, and be part of the change.
